Headquartered in Mayfair, AfterSportsConsultancy (ASC) offers extensive expertise in supporting established players through to the final stages of their careers and beyond. It delivers strategic solutions to ensure a seamless transition into post-football pursuits, including business advisory services, football punditry, management, media opportunities and coaching.
Since its inception, ASC has swiftly positioned itself as a leading agency, identifying, representing, and negotiating on behalf of some of the country’s finest young talent. Adopting a highly selective approach, the agency prioritizes quality over quantity, ensuring each client receives personalised attention and unparalleled support.
The company’s ethos extends beyond developing successful professional footballers; it strives to cultivate outstanding, responsible and well rounded individuals equipped with financial literacy to build and sustain generational wealth. By helping players build a solid understanding of wealth management, the agency empowers them to create financial security for them and their families.
